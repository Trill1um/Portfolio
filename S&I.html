Coding
I really love coding. Just the act of coding really gets my gears grinding. I could easily learn coding for hours on end and not even feel sick of it, unlike other subjects. This was totally an anomaly for me. I found out about coding through sheer chance, I mean i knew of coding back then but I didn’t knew coding. I just love turning my ideas into tangible programs that I get to manipulate and experiment on. If I wanted to start my studying day filled with dopamine, I would try to solve a Leetcode. Sure it would take a quarter of the day, but at least my brain is warmed up. The videos on the left are some of the personal projects I’ve worked on, highlighting my competency with coding. The first one is a Personal Budgeting thing using excel and VBA, the video shows how to use it and showcasing features like adding/deleting expenses, adding/deleting income/budget. This "program" also include an automatic updating pir chart to show the ratios between needs wants, and savings. The second one is just a Youtube Clone, I copied the Youtube website and styles, not one to one, but close enough to remble the real deal. I made this as an entry for the SCRIPT auditions. The last one was a game I made during highschool for a project (that has been cancelled by the way), but thankfully I've reporpused it for my presentation in NSTP. It also uses VBA as the langugage and powerpoint as the medium. All of these projects are not worth bragging about, I mean I’m still at an Intermediate level after all, but I still would love to track my progress, something to look back on.

Biking
This is probably one of my favorite hobbies to do in my free time. Because 1, it’s the only time I exercise, and 2, I get to explore places I couldn’t even go to normally. On a biking day, I would wake up at 4 am, do my preparations like warming up, carb loading, and checking up on my gear. Then by 5 am I would ride the day away. If my day wasn’t packed, I would ride until my legs gave out. Like literally, biking for 5-8 hours, going from one city to the next. Though this is probably unhealthy for me because I wouldn’t do exercise for like 3 months and get on the bike and ride for 5 hours.
